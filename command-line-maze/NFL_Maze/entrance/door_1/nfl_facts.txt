NFL QUICK FACTS

- The NFL was founded in 1920.
- There are 32 teams across two conferences.
- The Super Bowl is one of the most-watched sporting events annually.
- Teams play home games in a wide range of stadium types and climates.
- Franchise history often shapes fan identity more than current success.
